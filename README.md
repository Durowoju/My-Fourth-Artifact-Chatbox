### **Personal Value Statement on Navigating Human Bias**

As a leader in the technology and analytics field, I believe that understanding human bias is essential to creating fair and responsible systems. Bias shapes how people make decisions, interpret data, and interact with others, often without realizing it. My personal value is to lead with awareness, humility, and fairness. I want to create an environment where people feel respected, where decisions are made carefully, and where we take time to question our assumptions. Strong leadership requires being honest about blind spots and actively reducing the impact they have on teams, data, and decisions.

Human bias will always exist, but leaders have a responsibility to minimize its influence. Whether I am interpreting data, managing a project, or supporting a team, I value clear communication, diverse input, and continuous learning. These principles help people feel seen and help organizations make better choices.
